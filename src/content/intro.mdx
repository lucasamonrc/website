Welcome! My name is **Lucas**, and I'm a software engineer based in Austin, TX.

I'm a founding engineer at [Trinsic](https://trinsic.id), writing code primarily in **TypeScript** with **React**, and in **C#** with **ASP.NET**. We help people use digital credentials to get their identity verified online more quickly and securely.

I'm also a father, latter-day saint, and life-long learner.
